How Runway works

01
Pre-flight

Queue topics (one per line) in "Request topics." Each topic becomes a flight on the manifest.

02
Research & script

The pipeline researches the topic and drafts copy in your voice. Open the flight → Script tab to review. Click Clear script to approve — post-only flights skip voice and video.

03
Post & video (equal weight)

Edit channel-specific post copy on the Post tab. For video flights, preview the render on the Video tab. Hybrid flights use both tabs from one topic.

04
Cruise

Choose Deliver as on the Publish tab — text post, video, or both. Set a departure time, select destinations, then Depart now.

05
Weekly rhythm

Check Flight plan for AI-generated topic ideas and posting cadence. 10 minutes a week keeps the pipeline full.

What you edit vs. what Runway automates

You editHook, script, caption, departure time
Runway automatesResearch, voice recording, video rendering, publishing

FAQ

Why isn't my video ready yet?

Videos take 5–15 minutes after script is cleared. If it's been over 30 minutes, check the pipeline status tag at the top of the dashboard.

Can I change the hook after the video renders?

Yes — edit the hook in the Script tab and click "Re-render final" in the Video tab. It will re-render with the new text.

What does "Hold queue" mean?

Topics waiting to enter the pipeline. The pipeline picks them up automatically — nothing to do except wait.
