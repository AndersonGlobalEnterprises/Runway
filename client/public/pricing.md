# Pricing — AGE Runway

AGE Runway is a done-for-you content marketing and managed growth service for contractors and expert operators. All plans are monthly subscriptions and include voice clone, AI copy, video rendering, scheduled publishing, human QA review, and Flight Deck portal access. Posts and short-form videos count equally toward the monthly output.

## Starter
- Price: $497/month
- Output: 4 posts + 4 videos per month
- Platforms: 1 connected platform
- Includes: Voice clone + brand templates, Flight Deck portal access
- Best for: A single brand getting consistent weekly content live on one primary platform.

## Growth (Most popular)
- Price: $797/month
- Output: 8 posts + 8 videos per month
- Platforms: 3 connected platforms
- Includes: Everything in Starter, plus priority topic queue and multi-track pipeline
- Best for: Operators scaling reach across multiple channels with higher output volume.

## Scale
- Price: $1,297/month
- Output: 15 posts + 15 videos per month
- Platforms: 5 connected platforms
- Includes: Everything in Growth, plus dedicated onboarding and a custom posting calendar
- Best for: Brands treating content as a core acquisition channel across five platforms.

## Notes
- Billing: Monthly subscription via Stripe. Cancel anytime.
- Founding rates: Locked-in founding pricing available for early clients.
- Launch time: First posts typically live within about 48 hours of kickoff.
- Human review: Every post is reviewed by a human before it publishes.
- Contact: support@ageflowops.com
